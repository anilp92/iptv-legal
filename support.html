IPTV Watcher — Support

IPTV Watcher is a private, limited-audience client app for a self-hosted IPTV server. It provides no content or channels of its own: you need a working server operated by you or your household, plus an account on it, before the app can do anything.

Getting started

  1. Open the app. On first run it looks for your server on the home network and shows it as a card — or type the server address manually.
  2. Sign in with the account your server's operator created for you and pick your profile.
  3. Live TV, catch-up, recordings and your video library then work as configured on the server.

Common issues

  • "Server not found" on first run — make sure the phone is on the same Wi-Fi network as the server, or enter the address manually. Local-network permission must be allowed for automatic discovery.
  • Works at home, not elsewhere — away-from-home viewing requires your server's operator to have set up external access; check with them.
  • Playback stutters — the Diagnostics section in Settings shows connection details your operator can use to investigate.
  • Forgotten password / new device — accounts and devices are managed by your server's operator, not through this page.
